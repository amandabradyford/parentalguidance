About

We've taken 1+ reviews from Yelp's Data Challenge and tagged those written by parents.

We've done this by looking at the text of the Yelp reviews. If a person used a phrase like "my kids" (personal possessive pronoun + 'kid' or synonym) on one of their reviews, we tag them as a parent. Then we use that parent tag on all their other reviews.

With just this simple tagging we are able to tag over 20% of reviews as being written by parents.

Easier searching, more time to play!
